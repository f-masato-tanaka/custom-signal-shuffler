Oh, forgive me, forgive me! I beg you on my knees to forgive me! I deceived both myself and you; it was a dream... 
I am suffering for you today; forgive me, my God, forgive me!...
Do not blame me, for I have not changed towards you in the least; 
I told you that I would love you, and I do love you, I love you more than myself! 
Oh, my God, if only I could love you both at once! Oh, if only you were he!
He has arrived... Do not ask me why it happened so; I do not know myself. 
But he is here and he was waiting for me. God knows how I should like to be with you now! 
But tomorrow I am marrying him. He has returned to me. Do not be angry with me, remember me with fondness.
I have hurt you, but the love I feel for you is pure; I will keep your memory in my heart forever. 
Farewell, my friend, farewell! Forgive me...
